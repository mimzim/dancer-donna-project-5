Formed in 1971, the United Arab Emirates (UAE), is a country with 8.3 million people located in the south east Arabian Peninsula. It is
an oil rich country and is the third largest oil producer in the Persian Gulf region. Its terrain is mostly desert, with a hot, humid climate,
except for the cooler climate in the mountains to the east. 88% of the population is not UAE nationals.
