Palau

Flag of Palau

Palau of the Arts

A volcanic island nation located in the northern Pacific, east of the Philippines, Palau is a country with a population of 20,000 people. As a territory under
US administration, Palau gained its independence in 1993, making it the youngest and least populated nation. The climate is hot, tropical and humid.
Part of this nation are 20 large islands and 566 small islands.
