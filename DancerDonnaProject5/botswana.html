Botswana Nature

Botswana is a country of 2 million located in southern Africa. It was once part of Bechuanaland, a British protectorate, but gained independence
in 1966. The city of Gaborone is the capital. The country is known for its wilderness and diamond mines. Weather in Botswana is sub-tropical
with hot summers and warm winters.
